EDBT 2026 Best Paper Award

After careful consideration, the committee has decided to select the following paper for the EDBT 2026 Best Paper Award:


"In-memory Incremental Maintenance of Provenance Sketches"
authored by
Pengyuan Li (Illinois Institute of Technology), Boris Glavic (University of Illinois Chicago), Dieter Gawlick (Oracle Corporation), Vasudha Krishnaswamy (Oracle Corporation), Zhen Hua Liu (Oracle Corporation), Danica Porobic (Oracle Corporation), Xing Niu (Oracle Corporation)


The committee has also selected the following paper as Runner-Up for the Best Paper:


"Thunderbolt: Concurrent Smart Contract Execution with Nonblocking Reconfiguration for Sharded DAGs"
authored by
Junchao Chen (University of California, Davis), Alberto Sonnino (MystenLabs & University College London), Lefteris Kokoris-Kogias (MystenLabs), Mohammad Sadoghi (University of California, Davis)
