Moomin Museum

Date: Tuesday, March 24, 2026
Time: Anytime between 5.00 pm and 7.00 pm
Location: Tampere Hall, Yliopistonkatu 55, Tampere, Finland

The Moomin Museum is an experiential art museum for Moomin fans and art lovers of all ages. Welcome Reception at the Old City Hall

Date: Tuesday, March 24, 2026
Time: 7.00 pm
Location: Keskustori 10, 33210 Tampere, Finland

City hospitality in Tampere begins with an official reception at the Old City Hall! The stunning Neo-Renaissance-style Tampere City Hall is located on the Central Square and was designed by architect Georg Schreck and completed in 1890. Over the years, the building has played a significant role in the city’s history, serving as a center of administration and hosting many important events, including those during the General Strike of 1905 and the Finnish Civil War. Renovated twice, the Old City Hall now functions as a venue for official celebrations and receptions.


Social Dinner at Solo Sokos Hotel Torni

Date: Thursday, March 26, 2026
Time: 8.00 pm
Location: Ratapihankatu 43, 33100 Tampere, Finland

Rising in the heart of Tampere, the Solo Sokos Hotel Torni Tampere is the tallest hotel in Finland, offering stunning views over the city. This hotel is a tribute to the unique phenomena, legends, and stories of Tampere, providing the perfect setting for experiencing the city’s authentic charm.

The social gala dinner of EDBT/ICDT 2026 will take place at Paja Kongressi, located in the historic locomotive sheds within the hotel complex. The restaurant values fresh Finnish ingredients and local products, offering high-quality Nordic cuisine.

Before ending your evening, you are invited to visit the elegant Moro Sky Bar on the top floor of the hotel. Featuring a rooftop terrace and breathtaking panoramic views, Moro Sky Bar has become an attraction in itself. It is Tampere’s first sky bar and the highest bar in Finland. An elevator takes you directly from the hotel lobby to a height of 88 meters.
