EDBT 2026 Test of Time Award

Established in 2014, the Test-of-Time Award awarded by the Extended Database Technology (EDBT) Conference recognizes papers presented at EDBT Conferences that have had the most impact in terms of research, methodology, conceptual contribution, or transfer to practice over the past ten years.

After careful consideration, the committee has decided to select the following paper presented at EDBT 2016 as the EDBT Test-of-Time Award winner for 2026:

Storing and Analyzing Historical Graph Data at Scale
authored by Udayan Khurana and Amol Deshpande

This paper focuses on temporal graphs, a topic which has grown in importance since it was published. It builds on the authors' previous work, proposing both an index for accelerating the processing of temporal queries, as well as an analytics framework for scalable parallel execution of such queries. It is one of the most heavily cited papers from the 2016 EDBT conference, including citations from both within and beyond the database research community.
